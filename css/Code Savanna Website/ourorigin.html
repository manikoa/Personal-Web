    Our Origin Story





    The world is rapidly advancing, and technology is at the forefront of this change. It has become increasingly clear that the ability to effectively navigate the technological landscape is a critical skill for success in the modern world. However, in many developing countries such as Zambia, the disparity in access to technology and education remains a significant challenge, especially for the younger generation. That is why initiatives like Code Savanna are crucial in promoting socioeconomic development by providing computer-related knowledge and entrepreneurship skills to the youth in Zambia. Code Savanna plays a vital role in promoting socio-economic development in Zambia by providing computer-related knowledge and entrepreneurship skills to the youth, bridging the technology gap, and creating self-sufficient individuals who can make a positive impact in their communities.

    Code Savanna is a not-for-profit organization that aims to encourage, connect, and support the next generation of young leaders in technology and entrepreneurship in Zambia. Its primary objective is to promote socio-economic development by equipping the youth with the necessary skills and knowledge to become self-sufficient individuals who can make a positive impact in their communities.

    One of the primary ways in which Code Savanna achieves this goal is by offering coding lessons to children above the age of five. By exposing children to computer-related knowledge at an early age, Code Savanna hopes to ignite their interest in STEM and inspire them to pursue careers in technology. This approach is vital in bridging the technology gap and creating a level playing field for all children, regardless of their background.

    Furthermore, Code Savanna offers coding training to the public, including internship programs with hands-on projects geared towards empowerment. This approach provides an opportunity for young people to develop practical skills and gain experience in the technology industry. This training also offers the participants a chance to explore different options and create an environment that allows them to develop personal skills and potential interests.

    Additionally, Code Savanna provides mentorship, support, and empowerment to entrepreneurs, women, and small and medium-scale businesses. This initiative is critical in promoting entrepreneurship, which is an essential driver of economic growth in Zambia. By empowering these individuals and businesses, Code Savanna creates a ripple effect that leads to job creation, innovation, and socioeconomic development in the country.

    Code Savanna seeks to empower women to take up tech. In many developing countries, women are often marginalized and face significant barriers to accessing education and employment opportunities. Code Savanna recognizes the critical role that women can play in driving economic growth and promotes their involvement in technology and entrepreneurship. This approach creates a more inclusive society and promotes gender equality, which is essential for sustainable development. In conclusion, Code Savanna is a critical initiative in promoting socio-economic development in Zambia. Its approach of providing computer-related knowledge and entrepreneurship skills to the youth is vital in bridging the technology gap and creating self-sufficient individuals who can make a positive impact in their communities. By providing coding lessons, training, mentorship, and empowerment, Code Savanna creates a ripple effect that leads to job creation, innovation, and economic growth. Therefore, it is essential to support initiatives like Code Savanna in promoting sustainable development and creating a better future for the younger generation in Zambia.
