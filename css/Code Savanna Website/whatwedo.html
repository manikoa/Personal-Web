    MOTTO: Nurturing 21st Century Minds

    Mission

    Our focus is to encourage, connect, and support the next generation of young leaders in technology and entrepreneurship in Zambia with the aim of improving the country's socioeconomic development and creating self-sufficient individuals..

    Vision

    We want to create a more skilled and knowledgeable workforce, foster the growth of new businesses and job opportunities, and contribute to the development of a more resilient and self-reliant economy in Zambia..
